My name is Andrea Kafati, from Tegucigalpa, Honduras. My passion for nutrition and nurturing my body started at a very young age, thanks to my role model: my mom. My mom would only buy healthy and nutritious food in my house, and I could not understand why all of my friends ate differently than me. However, when I got to college I finally understood that this was more than just a way of eating- it was a lifestyle that allowed me to have a positive relationship with my body. In freshman year of college I decided that I would maintain this kind of lifestyle but update it a little by allowing myself to at times indulge in the typical cafeteria "junk food." As much as I thought this was leading me to have the most amazing college and freshman year experience, I came to the conclusion that I did not feel like myself and that this was slowly leading me to have a not so positive relationship with my body and with food. This led to a sudden switch in my mentality, and when I moved out of the dorms and into my first apartment I started buying healthy groceries, cooking my own food, and making the habit of eating at home whenever possible.
